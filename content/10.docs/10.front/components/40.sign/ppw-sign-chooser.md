# \<ppw-sign-chooser\>

Choose a signature provider.


# source prop

`source` prop is `accounts` or `providers`
(default is `accounts`).

Providers :

:ppw-sign-chooser{source="providers"}

Accounts :

:ppw-sign-chooser{source="accounts"}


# disable_no_sign prop

:ppw-sign-chooser{disable_no_sign=1}
